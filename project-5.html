A BOOK ABOUT CHAIRS

6 x 7 inch page

An accordion book project that explores how experimentation with form can transform everyday objects into timeless design. Combining thoughtful layouts with strong visual composition, this book highlights 5 iconic chairs and their relationship between structure, material, and human experience. Each spread is designed to reflect the character of the chair it features, creating a cohesive and engaging editorial piece. And when expanded, all chairs come together as one design.
