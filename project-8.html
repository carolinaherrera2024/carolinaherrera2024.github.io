CTRL BFA
Exhibition Invitation Card

5.375 x 10.625 inch

An invitation postcard for the BFA Graphic Design exhibition, titled “CTRL,” uses a limited palette of green, white, and black to create a subtle cyber-inspired feel. The layout is clean and structured, focusing on clarity while maintaining a bold visual identity. The design features a peekaboo opening effect, revealing additional exhibition information as the card is unfolded. This interaction adds a sense of discovery, making the invitation feel more engaging while remaining minimal and intentional.
