The Beauty
in Color

1920 x 1080 pixels, 1 minute and 50 seconds

The Beauty in Color is a video piece inspired by a poem of the same name, focusing on how easily the richness in our everyday surroundings can be overlooked. The video starts in black and white to reflect a routine, almost detached perspective, and gradually transitions into color as a way of rediscovering a sense of beauty. Rather than something suddenly appearing, the transition to color represents a shift in awareness that culminates—an invitation to slow down and notice what's been there all along.
