LENSCAPE
AR App

Lenscape is an AR-powered city exploration app that helps users discover local history, art, and landmarks while connecting with nearby businesses and people. Immersive AR interactions allow users to earn points that can be redeemed for real-world rewards and promotions, encouraging meaningful engagement with their surroundings. The interface is designed for ease-of-use and to evoke an urban aesthetic.
