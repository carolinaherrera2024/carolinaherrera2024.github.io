THE RISE OF INFLATION
Editorial Spread

This editorial spread visualizes inflation as both a financial concept and a lived experience, using expressive typography, pacing, and hierarchy to guide readers through the emotional and societal impact of rising costs. The design balances clarity with visual tension, mirroring the instability that inflation creates in everyday life. A custom digital collage introduces the tension of financial instability. Through layout, scale, and tone, the project transforms data-driven subject matter into an engaging, human-centered narrative.
