THE RISE OF INFLATION
Editorial Spread

6.45 x 9.25 inch page

“The Rise of Inflation” started as a hands-on 3D collage made with different types of paper to build depth and texture. The process of layering and arranging the materials helped capture the chaotic and overwhelming feeling tied to rising costs. This piece was then brought into a digital format, where the composition was refined while keeping the original tactile quality intact. The final deisgn lives as part of an editorial spread, combining imagery and type to communicate the impact of inflation in a visually engaging way.
