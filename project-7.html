VECTOR STRIKE
Snowboard Series

65 x 10.4 inch

A triptych of snowboard designs inspired by the visual language of Russian Constructivism using bold geometry, a limited color palette, and strong sense of movement. Each snowboard design functions as both an individual composition and part of a larger system, emphasizing balance, rhythm, and directional flow across the three boards. Inspired by the movement's focus on structure and purpose, the designs translate sharp angles, layered forms, and high-contrast colors into a contemporary context. The result merges historical influence with modern action sports, reimagining the snowboard as both a functional object and a graphic surface for experimental, expressive design.
